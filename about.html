CREATIVE COMMONS
GLOBAL SUMMIT 2023

The global summit brings together thecommunity of experts, academics, and activists who comprise the Creative Commons affiliate network in a different country everyt two years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the commons, free culture and open knowledge.

Please contact us per email for any further question about CC global Summit 2023!
